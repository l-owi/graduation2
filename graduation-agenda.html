GRADUATION PARTY AGENDA

  1. Arrival and Welcome (30 minutes)

    • Guests Arrive: Welcome drinks and light snacks served.
    • Greeting and Registration: Guests sign a guest book and receive name tags.
    • Music: Soft background music to set the mood for mingling.

  2. Opening Remarks (15 minutes)

    • Host Introduction: A brief welcome speech by the master of ceremonies or a family member.
    • Acknowledgment: A thank-you to teachers, mentors, family, and friends

  3. Keynote Address (20 minutes)

    • Speech by a Keynote Speaker: A respected figure or someone meaningful to the graduate shares words of wisdom, encouragement, and inspiration.

  4. Graduates' Speeches (20 minutes)

    • Graduate Speech: One or two graduates share their journey, memories, and thank-yous.
    • Special Acknowledgments: Honoring individuals who helped along the way (teachers, family, friends).
